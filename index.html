About Me

Hello! I'm Dylan, an aspiring web developer.

I've loved computers and all things nerdy ever since I first discovered games on the Super Nintendo at just four years old.

My path to becoming a web developer has been an interesting one so far. After graduating with a Bachelor's degree in CIS from Cleveland State University, I began studying web development on my own using resources like FreeCodeCamp and Udemy.

I recently enrolled in the CWRU Web Development Bootcamp in order to meet fellow coders and to push my studies to the next level.

When I'm not busy coding or working at a retail pharmacy, I enjoy PC gaming, cooking/eating spicy food, and reading books on my Kindle. Oh, and I'm friends with a nefarious wonderful cat named Bwonsamdi.
